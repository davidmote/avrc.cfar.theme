In the Spotlight

U.S. News & World Report rated UCSD's graduate program in AIDS research eighth in the country - the highest rating of any program in the UCSD School of Medicine. The UCSD Center for AIDS Research (CFAR) contributes to the overall success of this program. In 2008-2009, CFAR service Cores contributed to 127 publications and abstracts, while CFAR investigators earned more than $47 million in NIH grant funding.

 

CFAR in the News

UCSD AIDS Research among the best in the U.S. On April 23, 2009, David Brenner, MD, Vice Chancellor of Health Sciences and Dean of the UCSD School of Medicine, announced that UCSD's AIDS research graduate program was rated eighth in the country.
